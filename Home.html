What is Masque of the Black Rose?

Masque of the Black Rose is a discord-server inspired by the the Riot's show Arcane built upon unity, resilience and growth. We desire to create a community where members can support and be comfortable with each other, aiming to build lasting friendships. Our community thrives on collaboration, respect, and shared progress - whether it's in-game or out, we uplift each other to reach our full potential. We welcome all who wished to be a part of our family - not bound by blood, but by trust, loyalty and respect for each other.

How to join the Server?

To be a part of our family, join our discord via socials.
